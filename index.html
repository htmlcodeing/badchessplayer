How to Play chess


Chess is fun and may be a challenging game.





Learning chess is not hard.


It only takes practice to be able to become efficient with the moves that you make. In chess there are rules that some people do not know. In the How to Play page I will teach to the basics that you need to know to learn how to play chess. Although you may think that it is hard to play chess, it is not hard at all to learn.
